AS DARK BLUE

AND PINK

OF SKIES

REFLECTED IN AN EVENING MUDFLAT

show in the shadow

of a kingfisher

flashing in

the mirrored ripples,

or as two dark eyes

stare across

a table

and for an instant

you forget

the thing

you are,

or as

a

YELLOW PANSY

when you discover

you are

A MAMMAL

PATRIOT.

These things

also are

the substrate

and the

chop

and

moil

of what we

swirl in.

—Michael McClure
